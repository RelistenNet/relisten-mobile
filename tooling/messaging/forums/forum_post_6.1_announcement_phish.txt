Relisten v6.1: Last.fm scrobbling, Google Cast, 4000+ artists, and a lot more

---

Hey everyone, we just released Relisten v6.1 — the app for streaming live recordings from phish.in and the Live Music Archive. It is rolling out now on [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/app/relisten-all-live-music/id715886886) and [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.relisten.android) with a bunch of stuff that was either missing or requested after the v6 rebuild.

**All 4000+ artists on archive.org's Live Music Archive are now available in the app** (in addition to every show on phish.in).

Here's what's new:

**Both platforms:**
* Google Cast: cast to Chromecast, Google TV, etc.
* Last.fm scrobbling: one of the most requested features after v6, it's back
* Shuffle and repeat: single track or full show
* Popular and trending: see what other people are listening to right now, discover shows and artists by popularity
* Refreshed player UI and tab bar

**iOS:**
* Full CarPlay integration: browse artists, years, shows, your library and play directly from your car — this was one of the most requested things that got dropped for the v6 launch
* Rebuilt audio engine: smoother gapless playback, better handling of seeks and skips, more reliable background audio and lock screen controls

**Android:**
* Basic Android Auto support: play/pause, skip, and lock screen controls from your car. Not as full-featured as CarPlay yet, but it's a start
* A ton of bug fixes and quality of life improvements: audio focus handling (Relisten now properly stops when another app like Spotify starts playing), crash fixes, faster interface, less unnecessary loading, and a new playback bar design

Plus general bug fixes across both platforms: source loading issues, artist navigation, and playback edge cases. We've also made some technical improvements that will let us ship updates quicker.

Things that are we looking at doing next (no particular order):
* Support for archive.org's Aadam Jacobs + taperssection collections support to cover additional artists and sources
* Playlists support and syncing favorites
* Relisten "radio"
* Search + cross artist song and venue tracking

As always, the app is free, ad-free, and open source. If you run into anything or have feedback/suggestions, drop it here or on the Relisten Discord https://discord.gg/TSaj6vU or Instagram @relistenapp
